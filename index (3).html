What it detects

Head tilt
Detects when your head rolls left or right beyond your calibrated baseline.
Head down
Catches chin-drop from looking down at your phone or keyboard.
Head up / back
Flags when your monitor is too low and you're craning upward.
Shoulder shrug
Tracks whether your shoulders are creeping up toward your ears.
Forward slouch
Measures face size growth and position to detect leaning into the screen.
Session scoring
Live posture score and colour-coded timeline show exactly when you went wrong.

How it works

1
Open the app
Launch and click Begin session. No account needed.
2
Calibrate
Sit upright, look at the screen, press C.
3
Work normally
The app alerts you whenever posture slips.
4
Review
Check your score and timeline to spot patterns.
